# Privacy Policy for Fleeting Time

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Summary

Fleeting Time respects your privacy completely. The app:
- Collects **no data** whatsoever
- Requires **no internet connection**
- Has **no analytics, tracking, or advertising**
- Stores all information **locally on your device only**

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Period.

Fleeting Time operates entirely offline. No personal information, usage data, or any other data is ever collected, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

## Data Storage

All data you enter into Fleeting Time (such as custom timers, life timer settings, and preferences) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

Fleeting Time does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. There are:
- No analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No crash reporting services
- No social media integrations

## Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data, Fleeting Time is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. However, given our commitment to privacy, we do not anticipate any changes.

# Datenschutzerklärung für Fleeting Time

**Zuletzt aktualisiert: Januar 2026**

## Zusammenfassung

Fleeting Time respektiert Ihre Privatsphäre vollständig. Die App:
- Sammelt **keinerlei Daten**
- Benötigt **keine Internetverbindung**
- Hat **keine Analyse-, Tracking- oder Werbefunktionen**

**Wir erfassen keine Daten.** Punkt.
